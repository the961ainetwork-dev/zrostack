Privacy & GDPR Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026 · zhive.xyz
🛡️ ZHIVE is fully GDPR compliant. We never sell your data, never use it to train AI models, and you can request deletion at any time.

1. Who we are

ZHIVE (zhive.xyz) is an AI-powered business platform for MENA entrepreneurs. Our registered address is Beirut, Lebanon. For privacy matters contact: privacy@zhive.xyz

2. Data we collect

  • Account information: name, email, password (hashed)
  • Business data you enter (financials, credit assessments, contracts)
  • Usage data: which apps you use, when, and how often
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe — we never store card details)

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and improve our AI services
  • To send important account notifications
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • We never sell your data to third parties
  • Your data is never used to train AI models

4. Your rights (GDPR)

  • Right to access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Right to portability: Export your data in JSON format
  • Right to rectification: Correct any inaccurate data
  • Right to object: Opt out of any data processing

To exercise any right, email privacy@zhive.xyz — we respond within 72 hours.

5. Data security

All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We use Supabase for database hosting with enterprise-grade security. API keys are stored in encrypted environment variables, never in code.

6. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for authentication. No tracking cookies, no advertising cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party tracking.

7. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon deletion request, all data is permanently erased within 30 days.

8. Contact

Privacy Officer: privacy@zhive.xyz
General: hello@zhive.xyz
